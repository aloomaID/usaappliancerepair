Amarillo, TX Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Amarillo (/ˌæməˈrɪloʊ/ AM-ə-RIL-oh; Spanish for 'yellow') is a city in the U.S. state of Texas and the seat of Potter County. It is the 14th-most populous city in Texas and the largest city in the Texas Panhandle. A portion of the city extends into Randall County.
