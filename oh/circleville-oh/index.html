Circleville, OH Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Circleville is a city in, and the county seat of, Pickaway County, Ohio, United States. The city is situated along the Scioto River, 25 miles (40 km) south of Columbus. The population was recorded to be 13,927 in the 2020 census. Circleville is known today as the host of the Circleville Pumpkin Show, an annual festival held since 1903.
